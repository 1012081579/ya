Walk with Me in Moonlight

Leon Knight

Come, walk with me in moonlight. We'll rejoice the close of day.

Talk with me as moonlight sparkles dreams before our way.


Draw closer in the moonlight; feel warmth within my arms ?

drink deep the cup of moonlight; drink deep the magic charms.


We'll learn poetry by moonlight (Hold back the falling moon).

We'll drink every drop of moonlight, Knowing the sun shall rise too soon.
